Projects

Sensitivity Evaluation of SiGe PNPN TFET Biosensors for Label-Free Detection

Advisor : Dr. K P Pradhan
November 2019 - May 2020

From a Point of Care (POC) perspective, studied different MOSFET and TFET biosensor architectures and modeled a Double-Gate PNPN - Tunnel FET biosensor using SILVACO ATLAS TCAD to analyze the electrical characteristics of the analyte conjugation in the nano-cavity. The proposed PNPN TFET biosensor with Si0.5Ge0.5 (EG = 0.78 eV) source material outperformed the conventional TEFT with Si (EG = 1.1 eV) source dopant in terms of the elevated Sensitivity and high rate of disease detection.


Design of Crystal Oscillator for Analog Front End IRNSS Receiver

Advisor : Dr. Rajesh Zele
May 2019 - October 2019

Designed a crystal oscillator in 65nm technology node, using Cadence Virtuoso, to provide a stable clock reference of 25.94MHz to the PLL block. The on-chip oscillator was designed using an off-chip 26MHz SMD Seam Sealing Crystal resonator owing to its high Quality Factor and frequency stability. The frequency of oscillation and the Phase Noise results were simulated and verified across different PVT corners using Cadence Spectre.


Design and Simulation of 4-channel audio mixer

Advisor : Dr. Binsu Kailath
May 2019 - October 2019

Designed a 2-stage differential Op-amp in 180nm technology node using Cadence Virtuoso and simulated the 4-channel audio mixer using the Opamp as input. The functional integrity was ensured by comparing the post layout waveform amplitude and frequency with the schematic results.


Efficient realization of the secure cryptographic SHA3-512 algorithm

Advisor : Dr. K P Pradhan
May 2018 - August 2018

Developed SHA3-512 code in Verilog to encrypt messages of variable length. It works on the Sponge Construction algorithm resistant to preimage and second preimage attacks. I implemented each step mappings (q, r, p, c, i) with a bit rate r = 1088 to obtain a unique and irreversible final hash value after encrypting a 4800 bits data through preprocessing and random permutation of Sponge Construction algorithm.


TECHLOCK - Fast and Reliable Automated Door Lock System

Advisor : Dr. Priyanka Kokil
May 2017 - June 2017

As a team of 3 members, we developed an Automatic Door Lock System prototype using Arduino UNO, RFID Module, RFID Reader, and RFID Tag. The system has a controller PC that contains the record of the check-in and check-out of the user. The RFID reader detects the RFID Tag swiped by the user, matches the profile on the database, and opens the door automatically. In case of a mismatch, the door remains locked, and the owner gets a warning message.
